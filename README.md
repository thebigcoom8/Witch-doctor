# Witch Doctor

## Why It Exists

Witch Doctor exists because everyone deserves access to accurate information — no matter who they are, where they live, or what their doctor thinks of their choices.

The reality is that people are already using drugs, already modifying their bodies, already making these decisions with or without good information. Witch Doctor doesn't exist to encourage any of that. It exists because the difference between having the right information and not having it can be the difference between life and death.

No judgment. No gatekeeping. Just the information people need to stay as safe as possible.

---

## What It Is

Witch Doctor is an AI powered chatbot built around one idea — meet people where they are. Tell it what you're doing, why, and how, and it will give you honest information on how to do it as safely as possible, along with alternatives worth knowing about. No lectures, no shame, just answers.

---

## What It Covers

Witch Doctor is still early in development, but the first release will cover three core topics:

- **DIY HRT** — hormone replacement therapy outside of traditional medical systems
- **Recreational drug use** — safer use practices, dosing, and what to watch out for
- **Drug & medication interactions** — what combines safely, what doesn't, and why

More topics are planned and will be added over time. If there's something you'd like to see covered, open an issue or check out the [contributing guide](https://github.com/thebigcoom8/Witch-doctor/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md).

---

## How To Use It & Privacy

Witch Doctor is available as a Discord bot, a web UI, and can be self hosted locally with a straightforward setup guide.

**Privacy**

The only data ever collected is the total number of prompts sent across all users — nothing that can be linked back to you in any way.

However if you use the Discord bot, Discord itself will retain your chat history and other account data as they normally would for any conversation. Witch Doctor has no access to this data and cannot see your queries through Discord. For full details on what Discord collects see [Discord Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy).

If privacy is a concern, self hosting is always an option — your queries never leave your own machine.

---

## Data Sources

Witch Doctor pulls its information from a few key sources that were picked because they're trusted, well maintained, and already used by harm reduction communities around the world.

- **TripSit** — one of the most respected harm reduction resources out there, covering drugs, interactions, and safer use practices
- **PubChem** — a database run by the US National Library of Medicine covering detailed chemical and substance data
- **MedlinePlus** — a resource from the US National Library of Medicine covering medications, supplements and interactions
- **NIDA** — the National Institute on Drug Abuse, a US government resource covering substance research and effects
- **DIY HRT Wiki** — a community built resource for people pursuing hormone replacement therapy outside of traditional medical systems

All sources are collected once and stored locally — Witch Doctor doesn't call out to these in real time. Data is reviewed and updated manually to keep quality high and nothing gets added without being checked first.

Know a source that's reliable and worth including? Open a ticket and we'll take a look.

---

## Disclaimer

Witch Doctor is not a replacement for professional help. The information here is carefully sourced and maintained but TBC8, the person behind this project, is not a doctor — and neither is a chatbot.

This project does not encourage any of the behaviors it covers. In fact where safer alternatives exist we'll always point you toward them. If you're pursuing DIY HRT we encourage you to go through a doctor if at all possible, or if that's not an option where you live, to consider moving somewhere it is. Witch Doctor exists because we know that's not always possible and people deserve good information regardless.

For recreational drug use we don't encourage anyone to use drugs, especially if you're dealing with mental health issues — the risks are real and we'll always be upfront about them.

We do our best to keep information accurate but we're not perfect. If something is wrong, out of date, or missing please open a ticket so we can fix it and stop it from affecting anyone else.

**If you or someone you know is experiencing a medical or mental health emergency, this is not the place. Call emergency services or a crisis line immediately.**

Witch Doctor is not responsible for any harm that results from information obtained through this project. We only ask that if something goes wrong you let us know — not to cover ourselves, but because we genuinely want to do better.
